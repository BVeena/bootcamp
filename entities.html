HTML Entities

Some characters are reserved in HTML. If you use the less than or greater than signs in your text, the browser might mix them with tags. Character entities are used to display reserved characters in HTML. Some other entities are ampersand, cent sign, dollar sign, diamond, pound sign, currency sign etc... Entities code will be in entities number or entities name.

  • Lessthan <
  • Greaterthan >
  • Pound Sign £
  • Currency Sign ¤
  • Cent Sign ¢
  • Ampersand &
  • Yuan Sign ¥
  • Copy Right Sign ©
  • Section Sign §
  • Registerd Sign ®
